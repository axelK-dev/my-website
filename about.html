About the Book Club

The FictionTown Book Club was founded as a way to bring readers together through shared stories, conversation, and community.

Our Mission

We believe reading is more meaningful when it is shared. Our mission is to create welcoming spaces for thoughtful discussion, connection, and exploration through literature.

Our Story

What began as a small weekly gathering at the Oak Street Library has grown into a group of friends that meets locally and travels together to literary events across the state.

What We Value

  • Inclusivity and open discussion
  • Lifelong learning
  • Community connection
